Our story

Dallas's Trusted Pest Control Company Since 2008

Our mission is to provide safe, effective, and environmentally responsible pest control to Dallas homeowners and businesses, ensuring peace of mind and a pest-free environment.

Tip Top Pest Control Dallas was founded in 2008 with a simple vision: to offer Dallas residents a better pest control experience. Starting as a small family operation, we quickly earned a reputation for reliability, transparency, and outstanding results. Our founder, a Dallas native, understood the unique pest challenges of the area and built a company focused on local expertise.

Over the years, we've grown into one of Dallas's most trusted pest control companies, serving thousands of homes and businesses. We've stayed true to our roots by treating every customer like family, using eco-friendly products, and standing behind our work with a 100% satisfaction guarantee. Today, our team of licensed technicians continues to protect Dallas from pests, one home at a time.
