Our story

Dallas’s Trusted Pest Control Team Since 2009

To protect Dallas homes and businesses from pests using safe, effective, and environmentally responsible methods, while delivering exceptional customer service.

Tip Top Pest Control Dallas was founded by a team of local pest experts who saw a need for honest, reliable service in the area. Starting with a single truck and a commitment to quality, we grew through word-of-mouth referrals from satisfied customers. Today, we serve thousands of properties across Dallas, from historic homes in Oak Lawn to new builds in Lake Highlands.

Our team stays up-to-date with the latest pest control innovations, attending regular training and earning certifications. We believe in treating every home like our own – with respect, thoroughness, and a focus on long-term solutions. When you call Tip Top, you get a neighbor who cares.
