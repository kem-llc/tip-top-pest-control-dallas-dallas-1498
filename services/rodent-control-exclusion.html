Rodent Control & Exclusion

Remove rats and mice from your home and seal entry points to keep them out for good.

Schedule this service

Overview

Rodents can spread disease and cause damage to wiring and insulation. Our rodent control service includes a thorough inspection, trapping, and exclusion to seal gaps and prevent re-entry. We also offer cleanup and sanitization of affected areas.

What's included

  • Humane trapping and removal
  • Exclusion services to seal all entry points
  • Cleanup and sanitization of droppings and nesting areas
  • Prevention tips and follow-up visits

How it works

1

Inspection

We identify rodent entry points, nesting sites, and signs of activity inside and outside your home.

2

Trapping & Removal

We set traps in strategic locations and remove captured rodents, disposing of them properly.

3

Exclusion & Prevention

We seal gaps, repair screens, and install door sweeps to prevent future entry, and provide sanitation services.

Common questions

How long does rodent control take?

Initial trapping and exclusion can take 1-2 weeks, but you'll see results quickly. We follow up until the problem is resolved.

Do you use poison?

We prefer non-poisonous methods like snap traps and exclusion to avoid risks to pets and children.
