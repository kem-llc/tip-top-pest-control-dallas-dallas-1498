Rodent Control

Humanely remove rats and mice and seal entry points to prevent future infestations.

Overview

Rodents can cause significant damage and health risks. Our rodent control service includes trapping, exclusion, and sanitation to fully resolve the problem and keep rodents out for good.

What's included

  • Humane trapping and removal
  • Comprehensive exclusion – sealing holes & gaps
  • Sanitation and cleanup of droppings/nests

How it works

1

Assessment

We locate rodent entry points, nests, and high-traffic areas.

2

Exclusion

We seal all potential entry points with durable materials.

3

Monitoring

We set traps and monitor activity until the infestation is gone.

We prefer non-toxic methods. Poison is only used as a last resort and with safety measures.
