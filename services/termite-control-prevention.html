Termite Control & Prevention

Protect your home from termite damage with our advanced detection and treatment solutions.

Schedule this service

Overview

Termites can cause significant structural damage to your home. Our termite services include thorough inspections, baiting systems, and liquid treatments to eliminate colonies and prevent future infestations. We use state-of-the-art technology to detect termites early and protect your investment.

What's included

  • Early detection with thermal imaging and moisture meters
  • Eco-friendly baiting systems that target the colony
  • Liquid barrier treatments for long-lasting protection
  • Annual inspections and monitoring plans

How it works

1

Inspection

We perform a detailed inspection of your property, including crawl spaces, foundations, and wood structures.

2

Treatment

Depending on the infestation, we install bait stations or apply a liquid termiticide around your home.

3

Monitoring

We regularly check bait stations and reapply treatments as needed to ensure ongoing protection.

Common questions

How do I know if I have termites?

Common signs include mud tubes, hollow-sounding wood, discarded wings, and frass (termite droppings). We offer free inspections.

How long does termite treatment last?

Liquid treatments can last 5-10 years, while bait systems require ongoing monitoring but are highly effective.
