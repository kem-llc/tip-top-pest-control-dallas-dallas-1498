Bed Bug Treatment

Effective bed bug elimination using heat treatments and eco-friendly insecticides.

Schedule this service

Overview

Bed bugs are notoriously difficult to eliminate, but our proven methods eradicate them at all life stages. We use a combination of heat treatment, steam, and targeted insecticides to treat infested areas. Our thorough process ensures bed bugs are gone for good.

What's included

  • Heat treatments that kill bed bugs and eggs in one visit
  • Chemical-free options available
  • Pre-treatment preparation guidance
  • Follow-up inspection to confirm elimination

How it works

1

Inspection

We use specially trained dogs and visual inspections to locate bed bug hiding spots in beds, furniture, and cracks.

2

Treatment

We apply heat treatment raising room temperatures to lethal levels, followed by targeted insecticide application.

3

Follow-Up

We schedule a follow-up inspection 2 weeks later to ensure no bed bugs remain and provide preventive advice.

Common questions

How long does bed bug treatment take?

Heat treatment typically takes 6-8 hours. Chemical treatments may require multiple visits over a few weeks.

Do I need to prepare my home?

Yes, we provide a detailed preparation checklist including washing linens, removing clutter, and moving furniture.
