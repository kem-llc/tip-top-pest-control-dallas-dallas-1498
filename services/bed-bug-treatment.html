Bed Bug Treatment

Fast, discreet bed bug elimination using heat and chemical treatments for total eradication.

Schedule this service

Overview

Bed bugs are notoriously difficult to remove. Our multi-pronged approach combines heat treatment, steam, and targeted insecticides to kill bed bugs at all life stages, including eggs.

What's included

  • Heat treatment kills bugs without chemicals
  • Thorough inspection of mattresses, furniture, and crevices
  • Follow-up visits to ensure complete eradication

How it works

1

Inspection

We use canine or visual inspection to locate all infested areas.

2

Treatment

We apply heat (120°F+) and/or chemical treatment to affected zones.

3

Verification

We return after 2 weeks to confirm no re-infestation.

Common questions

How many treatments are needed?

Most infestations require 1–2 treatments, with a follow-up inspection.

Do I need to leave during treatment?

For heat treatment, yes – we recommend vacating for 4–6 hours. Chemical treatment may allow you to stay.
